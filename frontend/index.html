Advanced — per-tool toggles
Per-tool grants (ADR-0053). Toggle a row to issue or revoke a grant covering ONLY that tool. Per-tool grants override the plugin- level grant via specificity-wins resolution (ADR-0053 D3): a per-tool grant at green on a plugin-level grant at yellow effectively lets just that one tool fire without approval. Posture (above) is the orthogonal global brake — flip to red to refuse all action tools without revoking anything.
